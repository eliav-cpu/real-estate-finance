# 03 - Approval Flow

## Purpose

This document defines how a custom payment schedule moves from sales request to internal approval.

## Roles

- Sales: prepares the client request and enters the proposed schedule.
- Sales Manager: checks commercial reasonability.
- Finance: validates the economic impact.
- Management: approves material deviations.

## Statuses

### Draft
The schedule was entered but not reviewed.

### Ready for Review
All required inputs exist and the schedule totals 100 percent.

### Approved
The custom schedule is economically neutral or within the allowed tolerance.

### Review Required
The custom schedule changes company economics and needs commercial review.

### Finance Approval Required
The custom schedule creates a material economic gap.

### Rejected
The schedule is incomplete, invalid, or commercially unacceptable.

## Required Evidence

Every approval should keep:

- unit identifier
- base shell price
- proposal date
- selected official plan
- custom payment schedule
- annual rate assumption
- adjusted shell price
- economic gap
- approver name
- approval date

## Governance Rule

The engine gives transparency. It does not replace judgment.
