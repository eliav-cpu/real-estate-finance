# 04 - Roadmap

## Phase 1 Documentation

Lock the business logic, calculation rules, data schema, approval flow, QA checklist and sample plans.

## Phase 2 Excel

Add separate TVE sheets to the King Tamar workbook. Do not overwrite the existing simulator.

## Phase 3 Google Sheets

Prepare an online version with editable inputs, formula ranges, dropdowns, dashboard summary and approval log.

## Phase 4 SaaS

Convert the engine into a reusable pricing service with API, database tables, calculation function, audit log and proposal export.

## Phase 5 Governance

Use the engine as a controlled company process for custom payment plan approval.
