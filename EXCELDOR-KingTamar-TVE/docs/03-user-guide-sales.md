# 03 - Sales User Guide

## Goal

This guide explains how a salesperson should use the TVE engine when a client requests a custom payment plan.

## Step 1 - Confirm The Unit

Confirm project, building, unit number, unit status, shell price, proposal date, and delivery date.

## Step 2 - Shell Only

The TVE input must be shell-only.

Excluded from TVE:

- finishing package
- furniture
- parking
- legal fees
- escort fees
- management fees

## Step 3 - Enter Custom Payment Plan

For each payment line, enter payment stage, payment type, payment date, and payment percent.

The total payment percentage must equal 100 percent.

## Step 4 - Review Result

The engine returns adjusted shell price, difference from official shell price, approval status, and internal approval text.

## Step 5 - Approval Flow

### OK
The plan is economically aligned with the official model.

### Review
The plan creates a small pricing gap. Sales manager review is required.

### CFO Approval
The plan creates a material economic difference. Do not send to client before company approval.

## Sales Rule

Custom payment models must be checked internally before client confirmation.

## Recommended Client Wording

The payment model may be adjusted subject to company approval. Any custom structure is checked financially because payment timing affects the economic value of the transaction.
