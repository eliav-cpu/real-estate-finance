# 02 - QA Checklist

## Model Checks

- TVE sheets stay separate from the original simulator.
- The engine uses shell price only.
- Finish package, furniture, parking and service fees stay outside the engine.
- Custom payment percentages must equal 100 percent.
- Payment dates must be valid.
- A custom plan identical to the official plan must return zero economic gap.

## Approval Checks

- OK: no material economic gap.
- Review: commercial review is needed.
- Finance Approval: custom timing changes company economics.

## User Checks

- Editable inputs are clearly marked.
- Formula cells are protected or visually separated.
- Output text is clear for sales, finance and management.
