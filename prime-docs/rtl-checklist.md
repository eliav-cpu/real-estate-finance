# RTL Checklist for PRIME Word Documents

Use this checklist before sending any Hebrew DOCX or PDF to a client.

1. Do not use automatic Word numbering.
2. Write section numbers manually as text.
3. Set every paragraph to right to left.
4. Set every table direction to right to left.
5. Set every table cell paragraph to right to left.
6. Keep checkboxes on the right side of the text.
7. Put prices and percentages in dedicated cells.
8. Keep English names such as SMART, COMPASS and INFINITY inside fixed cells.
9. Render the file to images before delivery.
10. Check that section numbers, bullets and checkboxes are on the right side.

For the marketing and strategy agreement, do not add apartment details. The agreement includes only the project name, service tracks, fees, legal protections and signatures.
