# PRIME Marketing Strategy Agreement V19

Scope: general marketing, strategy and accompaniment agreement.

Do not include apartment number, apartment price, floor, parking or unit area.

## Required sections

1. Header and agreement title.
2. Parties.
3. Recitals.
4. Section 1: Introduction.
5. Section 2: Buyer declarations.
6. Section 3: Company declarations.
7. Section 4: Company services.
8. Section 4.1: Track selection.
9. Section 4.2: Track comparison table.
10. Section 4.8: Profit sale mechanism, split into short sub sections.
11. Section 5: Fee and payment terms.
12. Section 6: Final provisions and jurisdiction.
13. Signatures.

## Track table

SMART: Full purchase accompaniment. Personal strategy meeting. Unit selection. Signing and purchase process. Lawyer fee, registration and rights included in the track. Fee: 23,600 NIS including VAT.

COMPASS: Banking, financing and money map. Includes SMART. Banking and finance coordination. Finance documents. Funding and banking connections when relevant. Money map and asset portfolio planning. Add-on: 5,500 NIS including VAT.

INFINITY: Capital route, relocation and VIP. Includes COMPASS. Personal capital route. Relocation or personal use review. Flight and hotel subject to track and purchase terms. Premium management accompaniment. Add-on: 6,000 NIS including VAT.

## UX rules

The track table is the visual center of the agreement.
Keep it simple, three columns only.
Use thin borders, soft header background and one bold price row.
Do not use heavy design blocks.

## Legal wording rules

Use simple legal Hebrew.
Keep all section numbers manual.
Keep all clauses right to left.
Avoid language that can be read as a promised result.
Any future sale service is optional, commercial and result-free.
